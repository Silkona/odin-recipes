Lasagne Recipe

Lasagna is one of the worldwide famous Italian dish. From North to South, it reminds us a lunch at mammaâ€™s, a gathering with family and friends or simply a Sunday meal.

Ingredients

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Bertoli Spaghetti Sauce
  • Butter
  • Ground Beef
  • No-boil Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350F
  2. Brown your beef
  3. Once browned, drain the grease
  4. Start to layer your lasagna with a quarter cup of pasta sauce on the base of the baking dish
  5. Next spread one third of your beef mixture on top of the sauce distributing evenly
  6. Spread a quarter of your cheese on next
  7. Next place your noodle sheets on top
  8. Place some dabs of butter on top of your noodles
  9. Repeat steps 6-8 two more times
  10. Once you have done all the layering, place another quarter cup of sauce on the noodles and sprinkle with cheese
  11. Bake for 40 minutes
  12. Serve
